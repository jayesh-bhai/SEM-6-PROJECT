What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers disguise themselves as trustworthy entities to steal sensitive information. They use deceptive emails and websites to steal your data and money.

Common Signs

Suspicious URLs, spelling errors, urgent requests, and unexpected attachments are common signs of phishing. Always verify before clicking links or downloading files.

How to Stay Safe

Verify sender identities, don't click suspicious links, use our URL checker, and keep your software updated to protect yourself from phishing attacks.
